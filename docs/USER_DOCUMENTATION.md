## 1. Getting Started

### The Interface
The application is divided into two intuitive zones:
1.  **The Strategy Deck (Left):** Where you input data, view insights, and control the AI.
2.  **The Live Canvas (Right):** A real-time preview of your professional document.

### Auto-Save & Reliability
*   **Peace of Mind:** Your work is automatically saved to your browser's secure local storage as you type.
*   **Resume Later:** Close the tab and return later; your draft will be exactly where you left it.
*   **Resetting:** Need a fresh start? Click the **Gear Icon** in the sidebar to open **Settings**, then select "Reset Application Data".

## 2. Crafting Your Resume

### Step 1: Core Strategy
1.  **Candidate Profile:** Paste your raw historyâ€”LinkedIn export, old CV text, or rough notes. The AI is smart enough to structure chaos.
2.  **Target Job (Crucial):** Paste the Job Description (JD) you are targeting.
3.  **Deep Grounding:** Click the **Search Icon** inside the JD box to let Gemini research real-time industry trends for that role. This injects 2025-relevant keywords into your profile.

### Step 2: Visual Identity
1.  **Template:** Choose **"Executive Suite (Gold)"** for banking/law/leadership, or **"Modern Tech"** for startups/engineering.
2.  **Typography:** Select Serif (Merriweather) for authority, or Sans (Inter) for clarity.
3.  **Headshot:** Click **"Generate 2K"** to create a professional AI headshot tailored to your role.

### Step 3: Initialize Generation
Click **"Initialize Generation"**. The AI (`gemini-3-pro`) will enter a "Thinking" phase (approx. 5-10 seconds) to analyze, structure, and optimize your data.

## 3. Analysis & Iteration (The "Pro" Workflow)

Once generated, look at the **Strategic Alignment** panel on the left.

*   **Match Score:** A 0-100% rating of how well you fit the JD.
*   **Gap Analysis:** The AI will flag specific weaknesses (e.g., "Missing Cloud Experience").
*   **The Magic Fix:** Click the **Lightbulb Icon** next to a gap. The AI will draft a specific bullet point or skill addition to fix it. Click **"Apply Fix"** to instantly regenerate your resume with that improvement.

*Tip: Iterate this process until your Match Score exceeds 85%.*

## 4. Finalizing & Exporting

### Cover Letter
Switch to the "Letter" tab in the sidebar. The AI will draft a compelling narrative connecting your unique strengths to the company's specific needs.

### Exporting
1.  Hover over the resume preview.
2.  Click the **Download Icon**.
3.  **Name Your File:** A popover will appear. Enter a professional filename (e.g., `Jane_Doe_SVP_Product`).
4.  Click **Download PDF** to get a high-fidelity, vector-quality document ready for submission.
