## The Paradigm Shift
The traditional job application process is broken. It is a manual, high-friction, low-conversion endeavor. Candidates send generic documents into "Black Hole" Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) and hope for the best.

**AI Resume Artisan** was built to disrupt this model. We don't just build resumes; we **engineer outcomes**.

---

## 1. The Core Problem
*   **The Semantic Gap:** Humans write resumes based on history. Machines (ATS) filter resumes based on future requirements (Keywords/Semantics). This mismatch causes 75% of qualified candidates to be rejected instantly.
*   **The "Blank Page" Paralysis:** Summarizing 10 years of experience into 1 page is cognitively expensive.
*   **Static Documents:** A resume for Netflix should not look like a resume for J.P. Morgan. Tailoring is necessary but tedious.

---

## 2. Our Solution: The "Artisan" Architecture

We treat the resume not as a static document, but as a **dynamic data payload** optimized for specific endpoints (Job Descriptions).

#### A. The "Thinking" Engine (Gemini 3.0 Pro)
